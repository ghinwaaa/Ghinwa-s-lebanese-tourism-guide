Discover the Top 5 locations of 2023

Lebanon, known as the "Switzerland of the Middle East", offers visitors an unforgettable experience with its diverse landscape, rich history, and vibrant culture. From the sparkling Mediterranean Sea to the majestic mountain ranges, there are many breathtaking locations to explore. Discover ancient ruins, indulge in delicious Lebanese cuisine, and immerse yourself in the bustling nightlife of Beirut.

Food and restaurants

Lebanese cuisine is a feast for the senses, with a rich mix of flavors, aromas, and textures that are sure to tantalize your taste buds. It is a reflection of Lebanon's diverse cultural heritage, blending influences from the Mediterranean, Middle Eastern, and European culinary traditions. Mezze, a collection of small dishes served as appetizers, is a hallmark of Lebanese cuisine, featuring items such as hummus, baba ghanoush, stuffed grape leaves, and kibbeh.

Beaches

Lebanon is blessed with some of the most beautiful and picturesque beaches in the Mediterranean. With crystal-clear waters, soft sandy shores, and a vibrant beach culture, Lebanon's beaches offer the perfect getaway for tourists looking for a relaxing vacation. Whether you want to soak up the sun, enjoy water sports, or simply take a stroll along the coast, there's something for everyone.
